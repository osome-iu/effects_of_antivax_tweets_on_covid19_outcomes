The file `monsted_mturk_vaxx_labels.tsv` contains antivax tweet classifier training data, which Monsted et al. labeled using Mechanical Turk. 
This data was developed for their paper titled "Algorithmic detection and analysis of vaccine-denialist sentiment clusters in social networks" (Tech. Rep. [doi:10.48550/arXiv.1905.12908](https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.1905.12908)). 
Restrictions apply to the availability of this data, which therefore is not publicly available. The data is, however, available from the authors upon reasonable request.
